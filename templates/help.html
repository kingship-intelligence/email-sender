Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up SMTP to send emails?

Go to Settings and fill in your SMTP host, port, username, and password. For Gmail, use smtp.gmail.com on port 587 with STARTTLS enabled, and an App Password (not your regular Gmail password). See the Tutorial for a full walkthrough.

What file types can I upload to extract emails?

MailBlast supports CSV, Excel (.xlsx/.xls), PDF, Word (.docx), and plain text files. The extractor scans every cell, paragraph, and page for valid email addresses.

How do I attach files to a campaign email?

In Step 2 (Compose Email), click Add files under the Attachments section. You can add multiple files of any type. They will be attached to every email sent in that campaign.

Why is the AI generation button not available?

AI generation requires an active Pro subscription and OpenAI to be configured on the server. If you have an active subscription and still see an error, contact support below.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Go to Settings and click Manage Billing to access the Stripe billing portal where you can update your payment method or cancel.

My emails are going to spam — what can I do?

Make sure your domain has SPF, DKIM, and DMARC DNS records configured. Use a reputable SMTP provider. Avoid spam trigger words in your subject and body. Send to engaged recipients only.

Is my SMTP password stored securely?

Yes. Your SMTP password is encrypted with AES-256 (via Fernet) before being stored in the database. It is never returned to the browser.

How many emails can I send per campaign?

There is no limit imposed by MailBlast — you can send to as many recipients as you like. Your SMTP provider may have its own daily or hourly rate limits.
