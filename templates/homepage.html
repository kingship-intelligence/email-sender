How RushMail works

Three steps from a file of contacts to a personalized campaign in flight.

  1. 1

    Extract your list

    Upload a file or paste a URL. We find every address and guess names where we can.

  2. 2

    Compose once

    Write or generate the email, drop in merge tags, attach files if you need them.

  3. 3

    Send or schedule

    Ship immediately with live progress, or set a recurring schedule and walk away.
