Tutorial

Step-by-step guides for every feature in RushMail

1

Configure SMTP

Connect your email account so RushMail can send on your behalf

RushMail sends emails through your own SMTP server — it never stores or proxies your messages. You'll need SMTP credentials from your email provider.

Gmail (recommended)

  1. In your Google Account, go to Security → 2-Step Verification and enable it if you haven't already.
  2. Then go to Security → App Passwords and generate a new app password for "Mail".
  3. In RushMail, open Settings and enter:
    • SMTP Host: smtp.gmail.com
    • Port: 587
    • Username: your full Gmail address
    • Password: the 16-character App Password
    • STARTTLS: checked ✓
  4. Click Save SMTP Settings.

Other providers

  • Outlook / Office 365: host smtp.office365.com, port 587, STARTTLS on
  • Yahoo: host smtp.mail.yahoo.com, port 465, SSL (uncheck STARTTLS)
  • Mailgun / SendGrid / SES: use the SMTP credentials from your provider dashboard
2

Extract Email Addresses

Pull emails from a file or any public webpage

Start a new campaign and you'll land on the Extract Emails step. There are two ways to build your recipient list:

From a file

  1. Click the Upload File tab (selected by default).
  2. Drag and drop a file onto the upload zone, or click browse to pick one from your computer.
  3. Supported formats: CSV, Excel (.xlsx/.xls), PDF, Word (.docx), and plain text files.
  4. RushMail will scan every cell, row, paragraph, and page for email addresses and display them as chips below.
  5. Click the × on any chip to remove an address you don't want.

From a webpage URL

  1. Click the Webpage URL tab.
  2. Paste the full URL of a public webpage (e.g. a directory or team page).
  3. Click Extract — RushMail will fetch the page and pull every email address from the visible text.

Once you have at least one email, click Next: Compose →.

3

Compose a Campaign

Write your email manually or generate it with AI

AI generation

  1. In the orange AI box, type a brief description of your campaign — e.g. "Promote our new project management tool to startup founders. Focus on time-saving."
  2. Give your campaign a name in the Campaign name field.
  3. Click ✨ Generate with AI. The subject and body fields will be filled automatically.
  4. Edit the generated text as needed before proceeding.

Manual composition

  1. Fill in the Subject line field.
  2. Write your email content in the Email body field.

Adding attachments

  1. Click 📎 Add files in the Attachments section.
  2. Select one or more files from your computer (any file type).
  3. The selected file names will appear in the list — click ✕ to remove any.
  4. Attachments will be included in every email sent by this campaign.

Click Next: Review → when ready.

4

Send & Review Results

Confirm everything looks right and fire off your campaign

Review step

The Review & Send panel shows a summary of:

  • The number of recipients
  • Your subject line
  • A preview of the email body
  • Any attachments you added
  • The SMTP account that will be used to send

If SMTP is not yet configured, you'll see a link to the Settings page. Set that up first before sending.

Sending

  1. Click 📨 Send Campaign.
  2. A live progress bar and log will appear, showing each email as it's sent or fails.
  3. When complete, a summary shows how many were delivered and how many failed.
  4. Click Back to Dashboard to see your full campaign history.

Viewing past campaigns

Open the Dashboard at any time to see all your past campaigns with delivery counts. Click View on any row to see the per-recipient status.
